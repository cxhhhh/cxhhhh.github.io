Impact of unhealthy habits on Health & COVID-19
Unhealthy habits such as excessive drinking, insufficient sleep, obesity and smoking
have a profound impact on physical and mental health, making us more vulnerable to COVID-19.
In this visualization, we will explore the impact of unhealthy habits on people's health and COVID-19.
Percentage of Vaccinated of each County in the US in 2020


The higher the percentage of vaccinated, the darker the color. Otherwise, the lighter the color, and the lower the percentage of vaccinated.

It can be seen from the map that more people in the east of the United States are vaccinated with the COVID-19 vaccine.

Use mouse hover or click to obtain more information.
COVID-19 Cases in the US in 2020


The pink dots represents the COVID-19 cases, and the map shows the distribution of COVID-19 cases.

The eastern United States has far more COVID-19 cases than the western United States.

Combined with the previous map, it can be seen that the more COVID-19 cases, the more people get vaccinated.
Number of Deaths in each State in the US in 2020


The more deaths, the darker the color. Otherwise, the lighter the color, the fewer deaths.

The figure shows that California, Texas, New York, and Florida have the highest number of deaths.

Sliding the slider from left to right can cause the graph to display the states with the lowest to highest number of deaths.
Population of the top 15 COVID-19 Deaths States in the US



This figure shows the population of the top 15 COVID-19 Deaths States in the US. Each color represents a different state.

California has the largest population, followed by New York and Texas.

Moving the mouse to view the population of each state.
